Event Features

Beauty Artist

Our skilled beauty artists are experts in creating flawless looks tailored to your event’s theme and your personal style. They specialize in enhancing natural features and providing stunning makeup and hairstyling services that ensure you look and feel radiant throughout the event. Whether you desire a classic, glamorous, or trendy look, our artists deliver professional results that exceed expectations.

Videographer and Photographer

Our dedicated team of videographers and photographers capture every moment of your event with creativity and precision. From candid shots to formal portraits, they use state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to produce high-quality videos and photographs that beautifully preserve the essence and emotions of your special day.

Flower Decoration

Transform your venue with our exquisite flower decoration services. Our talented floral designers create stunning arrangements and installations that enhance the ambiance and theme of your event. Whether you prefer classic elegance, rustic charm, or contemporary sophistication, we tailor our designs to reflect your vision and style.

Live Band Party

Elevate your event with the electrifying energy of live music performed by our talented bands. From soulful jazz to upbeat rock, our bands deliver captivating performances that create a lively and memorable atmosphere for your guests.

Stage Decoration

Make a statement with our exquisite stage decoration services that transform your event space into a captivating showcase. Our skilled decorators create bespoke stage designs tailored to your event theme and specifications. Whether it’s for a conference, awards ceremony, or gala dinner, our stage decorations enhance the visual appeal and atmosphere of the stage.
